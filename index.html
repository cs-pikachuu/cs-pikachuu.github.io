Haotian Hu

Georgia Institute of Technology

Big fan of Pokemon TCG

I’m currently a first-year Machine Learning Ph.D. student at the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department of Georgia Institute of Technology, working with Prof.Faramarz Fekri. Previously, I recieved my bachelor degree from University of Science and Technology of China, School of the Gifted Young.

My current research focuses on building efficient and reliable machine learning systems, specifically in the following topics: (i) Hypothesis generation with Large Language Models; (ii) Large Language Models Towards Reasoning; (iii) Deep Learning Theory;

I was fortunate to cooperate with Zhenyu Zhang@UT-Austin (Atlas Wang - VITA), Tianjin Huang@UOE, Yufei Ding@UCSD, Sitao Huang and Kai Zhong@UESTC.

You can find me through Google Scholar, Discord and Gmail.
